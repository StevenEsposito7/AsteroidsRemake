Instructions: Destroy all the asteroids without losing all your lives. Losing a life subtracts 1000 points from your score. Big asteroids are worth 100 points, medium asteroids are worth 200 points, and small asteroids are worth 300 points.

CONTROLS:
Move Left/Right: A and D keys, Left and Right keys, D-Pad Left and Right buttons, or Left Thumbstick X Axis
Move Forward: W key, Up key, D-Pad Up button, or Left Thumbstick Y Axis
Fire Laser: Spacebar, or Left, Right, Top, or Bottom gamepad face buttons
